YouTube Livestreams
Theater Mode
Welcome and thank you for installing YouTube Livestreams Theater Mode! Here are your first steps:
  • If you already have YouTube open, you need to reload the page for the extension to become active. If the extension is still not active, try left clicking the extension icon in the top right corner or right click it and choose 'Always allow on www.youtube.com'.
  • Open the options page to customize features, design and layout. When you change any options, they are instantly applied to your open YouTube tabs.
You can access everything you need (options page, link to donate, website & links to my socials) through the extension menu, which you can open by clicking the extension icon in the top right corner of your browser.
